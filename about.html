About Us

Welcome to Attendify! We are a team of passionate developers dedicated to creating the best platform for attendance management. Our mission is to make tracking attendance efficient and reliable for educational institutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Attendify started as a small project aimed at simplifying the attendance process for schools and universities. Over time, we grew into a full-fledged platform with advanced features like QR code scanning and real-time reporting.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide educators and students with a seamless experience to track attendance with accuracy and ease. We aim to make the process hassle-free and accessible for everyone.
